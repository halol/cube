    How it works?

    If you host your website on multiple servers, use Professional Load Balancing Service to manage DNS across multiple servers. Get started today by signing up for an account and setting up DNS with Professional Load Balancing Service.

    Our service will monitor your web servers to make sure they’re online and responding. When our failover monitor detects that one or more servers are having an error, it pulls the IP addresses of those servers out of the available list and instead serves your website from the remaining available servers. When your servers come back online, the IPs will be restored and the server will begin receiving traffic again. If all hosts are down, we auto-forward requests to a location you specify.
